OMO: Open Music Ontology (minimal bridge)

OMO is a minimal, non-authoritative bridge vocabulary to link cultural heritage / research metadata with common industry identifiers and roles (e.g., ISRC, ISWC, “featured performer”).

Namespace (prefix): omo: → https://dataobservatory.eu/ontology/omo#
